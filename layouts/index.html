Container vs container-fluid

.container

.CONTAINER WILL CONTAIN YOUR CONSTRAIN YOU CONTENT TO THE CENTER OF THE SCREEN.

.container-fluid

.CONTAINER-FLUID IS GREAT IF YOU WOULD LIKE YOUR CONTENT TO SPAN FROM EDGE TO EDGE OF THE DEVICE.

rows and columns

Rows are good at keeping elements on the same line. Every row is devided into 12 columns. These 12 columns can be divided in any way that fits your needs. They can also be explicitly defined to act a certain way in responce to the screen size.

.container

This is an example of row and columns in a container.

col-lg-4 col-md-6 col-12
col-lg-4 col-md-6 col-12
col-lg-4 col-md-6 col-12

.container-fluid

This is an example of row and columns in a container-fluid.

col-lg-4 col-md-6 col-12
col-lg-4 col-md-6 col-12
col-lg-4 col-md-6 col-12
